Our Services

Prison Visitations

We visit inmates, offering companionship and sharing the love of Christ. Our team listens to their stories, prays with them, and reminds them that they are not forgotten. These visits bring hope and encouragement, showing prisoners that redemption is possible.

Counseling & Prayer

Through one-on-one counseling and prayer, we help prisoners find peace and purpose. Our sessions focus on spiritual renewal, guiding them toward healing and reconciliation with God and their loved ones.

Family Support

We support the families of prisoners, offering emotional and spiritual care. By visiting families and providing counseling, we help them stay strong, fostering hope and unity during difficult times.
